Nutrition Facts

Nutrition is essential for overall health and wellness. A balanced diet provides the nutrients your body needs to function correctly. Discover the benefits of different nutrients and explore healthy meal options.

Proteins

Proteins are essential for muscle growth, immune function, and overall health. Include sources like lean meats, fish, eggs, and legumes in your diet.

  • Lean meats: Chicken, turkey
  • Legumes: Lentils, chickpeas
  • Fish: Salmon, tuna

Carbohydrates

Carbs are the bodyâ€™s primary energy source. Choose whole grains, fruits, and vegetables for a balanced intake.

  • Whole grains: Brown rice, quinoa
  • Fruits: Apples, berries
  • Vegetables: Sweet potatoes, carrots

Vitamins

Vitamins are vital for maintaining overall health, supporting your immune system, and providing energy. Include a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables to get a range of vitamins.

  • Vitamin A: Carrots, spinach
  • Vitamin C: Oranges, strawberries
  • Vitamin D: Eggs, fortified milk

Minerals

Minerals are important for bone health, nerve function, and oxygen transport. Include calcium-rich and iron-rich foods in your meals.

  • Calcium: Dairy products, leafy greens
  • Iron: Spinach, red meat
  • Potassium: Bananas, avocados

Healthy Fats

Healthy fats are crucial for heart health and hormone production. Incorporate sources like nuts, seeds, and avocados.

  • Monounsaturated fats: Olive oil, avocado
  • Omega-3 fatty acids: Salmon, flaxseeds
  • Polyunsaturated fats: Walnuts, chia seeds

Healthy Eating Tips

  • Eat a variety of whole foods from all food groups for a balanced diet.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Avoid processed foods, excessive sugar, and unhealthy fats.
  • Incorporate more fiber-rich foods to support digestion.
